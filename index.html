We Care for Trees

We are committed to caring for the trees growing along the roadside. Over time, we have observed that the tree guards surrounding these trees are causing harm to their growth. These guards, initially intended to protect the trees, end up restricting their natural expansion.

As the trees mature, we recognize the need to remove these guards to allow the trees to grow freely. We are actively working to cut these guards and leave them behind, giving the trees enough space to continue their healthy growth. Our team is dedicated to improving the environment, and we continue to monitor and maintain these roadside trees.
